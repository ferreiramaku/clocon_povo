Welcome to the

Clóçon Pôvô website

Clóçon Pôvô is an international network of santomeans associations throughout the world. Our main objective is to unite, strengthen and empower santomeans.

Subscribe to our newsletter

Know who we are

Find out the reasons why this network of associations exists.

To defend the interests of the santomean diaspora in the spirit of unity.

To develop cultural actions that promote and value the santomean identity.

To improve the technical-professional skills of Sao Tome in order to make them competitive in the international market.

Helping to improve the income and quality of life of santomeans.

To support entrepreneurship through a specific action program.

Areas of activity

Training

Our trainings aim at the technical-professional growth of our members. We prepare a professional development plan for you in order to maximize your potential and income.

Entrepreneurship

The time has come for you to make business responsibly and sustainably. Our entrepreneurship program will help you to identify your area of activity, prepare your business plan, manage your business and seek funding.

Employment

Culture

Culture is not static. It must evolve and be refined. It must be of sufficient quality to be consumed by anyone, regardless of its origin. That's our bet. We intend to promote the São Tomé and Príncipe cultural identity.

Sport

We intend to create sporting tournaments/championships at local, regional, national and international level. In addition to the benefits of intergenerational interaction, we will also be discovering and promoting new values and talents.

How to contribute?

Donations

Choose a project or an initiative and make your donation responsibly and conscientiously. By donating to our projects and initiatives, you can benefit from tax reductions of up to 66% of the donated amount. Contact us for more detailed information.

Fundraising

As a fundraiser, you will participate in our fundraising campaigns aimed at making our projects and initiatives viable. There are several modalities foreseen for this type of collaboration.

Volunteering

By becoming a volunteer you will be actively contributing to the materialization of our initiatives. Volunteers contribute with their know-how and experience in training projects, awareness raising, conferences, lectures and seminars, cooperation and social solidarity projects.
